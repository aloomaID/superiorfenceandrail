Patchogue, NY Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Patchogue (/ˈpætʃɒɡ/, PATCH-awg) is a village in Suffolk County, New York. The population was 12,408 at the time of the 2020 census. Patchogue is part of the town of Brookhaven, on the south shore of Long Island, adjoining Great South Bay. It is officially known as the Incorporated Village of Patchogue.
