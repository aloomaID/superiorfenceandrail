Eastman, GA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Eastman is a city in Dodge County, Georgia, United States. The population was 4,962 at the 2010 census. The city was named after William Pitt Eastman, a native of Massachusetts who purchased a large tract of land along the Macon and Brunswick Railroad, and settled a city on the site.
