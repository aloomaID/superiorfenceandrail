Fern Acres, HI Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Fern Acres is located on the eastern side of the island of Hawaii at 19°30′43″N 155°5′24″W (19.511846, -155.089878). It is bordered to the east by Hawaiian Acres, to the north by Mountain View, and to the west by Eden Roc. To the south is the Puna Forest Reserve.
