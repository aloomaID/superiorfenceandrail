Kayenta (Navajo: Tó Dínéeshzheeʼ) is a U.S. town which is part of the Navajo Nation and is in Navajo County, Arizona, United States. Established November 13, 1986, the Kayenta Township is the only 'township' existing under the laws of the Navajo Nation, making it unique in this way.
