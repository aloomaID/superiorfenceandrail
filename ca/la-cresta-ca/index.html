La Cresta, CA Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

La Cresta is an unincorporated community in the Santa Rosa Plateau region in Riverside County, California, United States. It is situated west of Murrieta, north of Temecula and the San Diego County Border, and east of Orange County. It is centrally located approximately 55 miles from Downtown San Diego, 65 miles from Downtown Los Angeles, and 15 miles from the Pacific Ocean. The population of La Cresta is approximately 2,860.
