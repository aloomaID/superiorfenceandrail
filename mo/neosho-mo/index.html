Neosho (/niːˈoʊʃoʊ/; originally Siouan pronunciation: or Siouan pronunciation: ) is the most populous city in Newton County, Missouri, United States, which it serves as the county seat. With a population of 12,590 as of the 2020 census, the city is a part of the Joplin, Missouri Metropolitan Statistical Area, a region with an estimated 176,849 (2011) residents.
