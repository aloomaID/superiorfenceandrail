Sicklerville, NJ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Sicklerville is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) located within Gloucester Township, Washington Township, and Winslow Township in Camden County and Gloucester County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. It was founded in 1851 by John Sickler, who lived in the area when the present-day town was located within Gloucester Township. The area is served as United States Postal Service ZIP Code 08081.
