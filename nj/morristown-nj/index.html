Morristown, NJ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Morristown (/ˈmɒrɪstaʊn/) is a town and the county seat of Morris County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. Morristown has been called 'the military capital of the American Revolution' because of its strategic role in the war for independence from Great Britain. Morristown's history is visible in a variety of locations that collectively make up Morristown National Historical Park, the country's first National Historical Park.
